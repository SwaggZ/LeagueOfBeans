# LeagueOfBeans
Welcome to League of Beans, a fan-inspired FPS shooter game that brings the iconic champions from League of Legends into the world of fast-paced first-person action! Immerse yourself in the thrilling battles, each character featuring unique abilities and playstyles reminiscent of your favorite League champions.

This project is a labor of love by developer Dev Dambel, who has skillfully crafted an engaging and dynamic gaming experience. Dive into the intense battles, explore vibrant arenas, and unleash the power of your chosen champion in this unofficial, unlicensed fan project.

Features
Unique Champions: Play as characters inspired by League of Legends champions, each with distinct abilities and weapons.

Fast-Paced FPS Action: Experience the excitement of intense first-person shooter gameplay with a League of Legends twist.

Open Source: This project is open source, allowing the community to contribute, modify, and enhance the game.

Disclaimer
League of Beans is a fan project created by Dev Dambel and is not officially licensed by Riot Games. All references to League of Legends, including characters and lore, are used in a transformative manner to pay homage to the original work.
